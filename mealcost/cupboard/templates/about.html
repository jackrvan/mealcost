About the site

This site was designed by Jack Vanier after I started making granola bars from scratch for health reasons and wondered if I was also saving money this way. It is designed to tell you how much recipes cost to make. Still some pain points such as having to input all your food and prices manually. A few ways to mitigate this could be to parse some superstore websites for this data. Another pain point is needing to enter in the ingredients for the recipes. May be nice to parse online recipes for ingredients and import them.
